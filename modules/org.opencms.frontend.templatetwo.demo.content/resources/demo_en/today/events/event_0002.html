<![CDATA[New Braunfels Wurstfestival]]> Frizzling Brats in Texas: New Braunsfeld  Wurstfest  from 10/31 thru 09/11/2008

]]> Americans know how to frizzle a good "Bratwurst", too: In Autum, german visitors could attend a wellknown party in the heart of Texas.

Each November, the city of New Braunfels is throwing the "Wurstfest" and the inhabitants of the cozy city are showing for ten days in a row with traditional music, hearty food and beer what texans understand as typical german or bavarian. This year, the wurstfest is taking place from October, 31 thru November, 9.

The event itself can be seen as traditional, since it is now in its 45th year, already. With approximately 50 booths selling souvenirs, hearty snacks and a beer tent, the beer fest attracts up to 75.000 visitors each year.

The wurstfest was brought by german immigrants a long time ago, and offers everything typically seen as "bavarian" world-wide, till today.
