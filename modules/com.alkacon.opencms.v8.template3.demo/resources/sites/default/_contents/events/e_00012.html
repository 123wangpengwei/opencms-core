<![CDATA[Seasonal Gardens at Mainau Island]]> While this year's motto at the mainau is "Grandfather's Garden Bench" - Memories of bygone times - the Seasonal Gardens 2011 follow the theme "brave new world". They have been planned by 12 students of landscape architecture within a 2 day workshop. A jury then picked the top 5 concepts that could be put into practice. They have been given names as "It's your choice", "It's in your hands" or even "look up".
