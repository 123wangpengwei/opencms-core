<![CDATA[Floristika 2008 in Ober-Ammergau]]> Floristika 2008 in Dresden from July, 5 to July, 6

]]> 3.000 visitors came to this year's Floristika, which were arranged by the Chamber of commerce and industry, as well as the National Association Saxony of the union of German florists.  The Floristika took place from July, 5 to July, 6.

 

In the public fair, the visitors could admire the examination works of the florists out of the administrative region Dresden.

Especially wedding-, table-, and funeral decoration were exposed. Out of 57 examinees, at least 55 passed the florist examination. Even the grade "sehr gut" (A) was assigned three times.

The examination works- in this case georgeous bouquets- were auctioned for a good cause. The auction proceeds were donated to the Botanic Garden in Blumefelde.

 

The supporting programme consisted of a flower market and an extra exhibition "Lily". Additionally, the contest "crude bouquet" took place which t he florists Rosa Blume and Summer Begonie won.
