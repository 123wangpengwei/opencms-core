<![CDATA[Flower workshop in Bonn, Germany]]> September 25, 2008

Flower bonding is an art. Who wants to know how it works can try it under professional instruction in a flower workshop at Thursday, September 25, 2008 in Bonn. The workshop is free and takes place from 3 PM to 6 PM in the foyer of the Stadthaus, Berliner Platz 2 in Bonn, Germany.
