]]> Berlin - In the hope of a bloomy summer trade lots of German nurseries offer their customers an All- Inclusive Package.

Beside an extensive client counselling, which flowertype most suitable for the customer is, the nurseries offer also the delivery and implementation of the selected plants. After a successful pollination of the nursery-owned bees, the nursery control the single growing stages and work against vermin.

This service allows people without a "green thumb" to have a beautiful garden. In urban areas this service is also offered for balcony plants.

Berlin nursery foreman, Arno Amaryllis, is one of the first who offer this all-inclusive service. Competitors enlarged this service, thus they cull the flowers and deliver these as a bouquet.
