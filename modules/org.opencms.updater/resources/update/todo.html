OpenCms version: 6.1.8

Press here to open this list in a new window for printing.

Completing the update

Restart the servlet container.
Login to OpenCms and switch to the Adminstration view. Select "Flex Cache Adminstration" and press the "Purge JSP respository" button there.
It could be that you will need to clear the cache of your browser, to update images, styles and/or javascript code.
After this, the update process has been completed.
