Sample style sheet variation

This page demonstrates the style variation capabilities of the "templateone" template. You can exchange the background images and also alter the color scheme. You can also easily modify the links on the top navigation and select various options regarding navigation display.

Most of the styles are configured in the files of a folder named "_configuration". You can have multiple site "areas" that share a common configuration.
